## ðŸ—ï¸ Managing Cells

Use these to structure your notebook without reaching for your mouse.

* **Add Cell Above:** `Ctrl + M + A`
* **Add Cell Below:** `Ctrl + M + B`
* **Delete Cell:** `Ctrl + M + D`
* **Undo Last Cell Action:** `Ctrl + M + Z`
* **Convert to Code Cell:** `Ctrl + M + Y`
* **Convert to Text (Markdown):** `Ctrl + M + M`
* **Move Cell Up/Down:** `Ctrl + M + K` / `Ctrl + M + J`
